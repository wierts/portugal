The Complete 7-Day Travel Guide

From Porto's literary charm to Lisbon's vibrant energy, via wine valleys, medieval towns, and dramatic cliffs. Your complete guide to Portugal's magic.

Your 7-Day Journey Overview

Welcome to Portugal!
This 7-day itinerary takes you through Portugal's most enchanting destinations. Beginning in Porto, a city steeped in history and home to the world's finest port wine, you'll journey through terraced vineyards of the Douro Valley, discover colorful coastal villages, explore medieval fortified towns, visit romantic mountaintop palaces in misty Sintra, and finish in Lisbon's vibrant charm.

What Makes This Journey Special:
✓ Perfect blend of culture, nature, and gastronomy
✓ Boutique hotel accommodations in each region
✓ Mix of famous attractions and hidden gems
✓ Wine tastings from premier quintas
✓ Medieval architecture and UNESCO sites
✓ Dramatic coastal scenery
✓ World-class food and wine experiences

Practical Information

📅 Best Time to Visit

April-May: Perfect weather 18-24°C, flowers, fewer crowds. Ideal!
Sept-Oct: Warm 20-26°C, sunny, excellent.
June-Aug: Hot 25-32°C, peak season, book ahead.
Nov-Mar: Cool 8-15°C, peaceful, occasional rain.

🚗 Transportation

Car Rental: €30-50/day
Roads: Well-maintained, drive right side
Speed Limits: 50 km/h city, 90 regional, 120 motorway
Parking: Good in towns, challenging in cities

💰 Budget Estimates

Accommodation: €100-180/night (mid-range)
Meals: €40-80/day
Attractions: €100-150 total
7-Day Total: €1,715-2,870/person

🍷 Food & Wine

Port Wine: €5-15/glass
Douro Wine: €8-20/bottle
Seafood: €15-30
Lunch: Better value than dinner

📸 Photography

Golden Hour: 1 hour before/after sunrise/sunset
Best Locations: Dom Luís Bridge, Douro terraces, Costa Nova, Pena Palace, Graça viewpoint
Early Morning: Fewer crowds, soft light

✅ Essential Bookings

3 Months Ahead: Accommodation, Palácio da Pena
2 Weeks Ahead: Graham's Port Lodge, Quinta do Pôpa
1 Week Ahead: Douro River Cruise
